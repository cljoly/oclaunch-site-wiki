### Prerequisite You have followed instructions on the . ### Let's start To use OcLaunch with Taskwarrior, simply add what you want. For example 

{
    "progs":
        [
            "task +rdv",
            "task ls"
        ],
    "settings":
    [
    ]
}
That's it ! Now, each time you open a terminal (since `oclaunch` is in your bashrc), it will remind you your tasks.
